Your Career Partner

The PCET Placement Cell

At PCET, we believe that success lies not just in academic excellence but also in providing students with the best opportunities to kickstart their careers. Our Placement Cell is the driving force behind our students’ professional growth, committed to connecting them with top-notch companies and organizations.

The PCET Placement Cell takes pride in its robust network of industry contacts, which enables us to facilitate campus interviews and job fairs, inviting renowned recruiters from various sectors. Our team of experienced placement officers works closely with students, guiding them through resume building, interview preparation, and soft skills development. We believe that every student has unique talents and aspirations, and our personalized approach ensures that each one receives the support they need to achieve their career goals.

We take immense pride in our successful track record of placing students in reputed companies across diverse domains. At PCET, we go beyond academics to ensure that our students are industry-ready, making us a preferred destination for recruiters and a stepping stone for our students’ professional success.

Comprehensive Placement Training

PCET is a leading institution committed to nurturing future leaders and empowering students with comprehensive education and skills. Our Placement Training program is designed to equip students with the necessary tools and knowledge to excel in the competitive job market.

At PCET, we understand the importance of bridging the gap between academia and industry demands. Our Placement Training program focuses on enhancing students’ employability by offering a wide range of workshops, seminars and mock interview sessions.

We have seasoned professionals and industry experts who guide students through the entire placement process, from resume building to developing essential soft skills like communication, teamwork and problem-solving. With a strong network of esteemed recruiters and tie-ups with various industries, our Placement Training program has an outstanding track record of placing students in top-notch companies.
