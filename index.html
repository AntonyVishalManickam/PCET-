About PCET

Shaping the Innovators and Leaders of Tomorrow.

Park College of Engineering & Technology (PCET), one of the Best Engineering Colleges in Coimbatore, Tamilnadu, is affiliated with Anna University, Chennai and approved by AICTE, Delhi. The college is a part of Park Institutions which over its 50+ years of existence has contributed immensely to the lives of aspiring engineers.

We focus on providing technical excellence, imbibing the qualities of entrepreneurship & grooming leaders who are ready for the digital future.
