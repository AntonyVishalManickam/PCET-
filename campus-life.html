    Campus Life

    Discover your home away from home at PCET

    Our Facilities

    A World-Class Learning Environment

    Located on a sprawling 30 acres of land surrounded by green fields and coconut groves, PCET provides an ideal, pollution-free atmosphere perfectly designed for living, learning, and collaborating.

    Knowledge Hub

    Central Library

    The PCET Central Library is the heart of our academic community. Spread across a vast area, it houses an extensive collection of books, international journals, research papers, and digital media catering to all branches of engineering and management.

    • Over 50,000 volumes of books
    • Access to IEEE, Springer, and DELNET digital libraries
    • Quiet reading halls and dedicated reference sections
    • Fully automated book issue and return system
    Accommodation

    Hostel Facilities

    We understand that a comfortable living environment is crucial for academic success. PCET offers separate, secure, and well-furnished hostels for boys and girls within the campus premises. The hostels are designed to feel like a home away from home.

    • Spacious, well-ventilated rooms
    • Hygienic mess serving nutritious vegetarian & non-vegetarian food
    • 24/7 security with CCTV surveillance
    • Recreation rooms with TV and indoor games
    Physical Education

    Sports & Games

    At PCET, we emphasize the physical well-being of our students just as much as their academic growth. Our campus boasts excellent sporting infrastructure to encourage students to participate in various athletic and sports activities at regional and national levels.

    • Expansive grounds for Cricket, Football, and Athletics
    • Well-maintained courts for Basketball, Volleyball, and Tennis
    • Indoor sports complex for Badminton, Table Tennis, and Chess
    • Fully equipped modern gymnasium
